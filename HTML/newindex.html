About Us

BlueWave Resorts by Meritas is situated in the midst of a peaceful vicinity of Lonavala. Surrounded by greenery and freshness, this resort is the perfect destination for a much needed getaway. Spread over 4 wings, Countryside is a mid-scale resort with 31 rooms. Every room is provided with amenities such as air conditioning, toiletries, luggage rack, wardrobe, LED TV, writing desk and much more. Countryside Resort is facilitated with modern yet contemporary interiors for a peaceful and relaxed stay. Facilities like Swimming Pool, Jacuzzi, In-Door and Out-Door games are accessible to our resident guests free of cost. While on holiday, one must binge and the resort’s restaurant has a wide range to offer from Indian, Tandoori, Oriental and Fast Food delicacies. The guest also has an option of In-Room Dining to enjoy a meal in the comfort of one’s room.
